Marley My Pet Dog!

I am Joy and Marley is my pet dog,i'd also say Marley is my Miracle. Marley and Joy didn't meet in a good way,it was rather in a tragic scene,I met Marley in the big city(adventure city),where he was abandoned as a puppy.

I first saw Marley crossing a really busy road and i watched him almost hit by a van, I took it to myself to stop the car from hitting Marley and at that moment i didn't see a puppy,i saw a brave dog in Marley and So i decided to take Marley in.

It's been 7years now with Marley and i can't say i regret taking him in.Marley is a labrador retreiver. Unlike other Dogs Marley isn't a cute,shy lovely dog,he is instead husky,social,and big dog.

PS;Marley would spend alot of his time chewing off pens ,tearing couches and running after other dogs in th neighbourhood
